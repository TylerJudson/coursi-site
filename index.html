Everything You Need to Succeed

Coursi brings together course management and AI-powered tutoring in one seamless experience.

Assignment Sync

Automatically sync assignments, grades, and announcements from Canvas LMS and BYU Learning Suite. See everything in a unified dashboard with smart deadline tracking.

AI Chat Tutor

Ask questions about your course materials and get instant, context-aware answers with source citations. Powered by RAG technology with support for OpenAI, Anthropic, and Google models.

Smart Documents

Automatically process and index PDFs, Word documents, PowerPoint slides, and Excel files. Upload your own materials or let Coursi sync them from your courses.

Multi-Platform

Native apps for iOS, Android, Windows, and macOS. A consistent, high-performance experience on whichever device you prefer.

Cloud Sync

Sync your data across up to 5 devices with secure magic-link authentication. Start studying on your laptop and pick up right where you left off on your phone.

Privacy First

Your course data stays local on your device. LMS credentials are stored in encrypted secure storage and never sent to our servers. Bring your own API keys for full control.

How It Works

Get up and running in minutes. Three simple steps to transform your study workflow.

1

Connect Your Courses

Link your Canvas LMS account with an API token and sign in to BYU Learning Suite. Coursi handles the rest.

2

Sync Everything

Coursi automatically pulls in your courses, assignments, announcements, grades, documents, and syllabi.

3

Ask the AI

Chat with an AI tutor that understands your actual course materials. Get cited answers, study help, and concept explanations instantly.

Intelligent AI Tutoring

Coursi's AI chat goes beyond generic answers. It uses Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) to search your actual course documents and provide responses grounded in your specific materials.

Source Citations

Every answer includes citations linking back to the exact documents and passages used. Verify the AI's response against your original materials with a single tap.

Multiple AI Providers

Choose from OpenAI (GPT-4o), Anthropic (Claude), or Google (Gemini). Switch between models on the fly and find the one that works best for your subject.

Smart Suggested Prompts

Not sure where to start? Use built-in prompts to explain concepts, summarize readings, quiz yourself on material, or even pre-grade an assignment before submission.

Persistent Conversations

Your chat history is saved and synced across devices. Edit previous messages, regenerate responses, and pick up study sessions right where you left off.

Powerful Document Processing

Coursi doesn't just store your documents — it understands them. Every file is processed, chunked, and embedded into a vector database for instant semantic search.

Auto-Sync from Courses

Documents are automatically pulled from Canvas modules, Learning Suite content pages, syllabi, and even external links referenced in your courses. No manual uploading needed.

Multi-Format Support

Process PDFs, Word documents (DOCX), PowerPoint presentations (PPTX), Excel spreadsheets (XLSX), plain text, Markdown, and HTML. Upload your own study materials too.

Vector Search

Documents are semantically chunked and embedded using OpenAI's embedding models, then stored in a local HNSW vector index for lightning-fast similarity search — all on your device.
